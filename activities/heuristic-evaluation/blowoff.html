Heuristic Evaluation on BlowOff.com

In this activity, you will perform a heuristic evaluation of BlowOff.com.
Evaluate THREE parts of the site, filling out the form for each usability problem you find on:

  • the home page
  • one product page (e.g., BlowOff Duster)
  • the checkout page

You'll use Nielsen's Heuristics to perform your evaluation.

  1. Match the real world
  2. Consistency & standards
  3. Help & documentation
  4. User control & freedom
  5. Visibility of system status
  6. Flexibility & efficiency
  7. Error prevention
  8. Recognition, not recall
  9. Error reporting, diagnosis, and recovery
  10. Aesthetic & minimalist design
