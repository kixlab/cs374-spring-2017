17: Choosing Logo Fonts

Bet you don't have a logo for your project yet. Choosing a font for your logo is a difficult process, so why not have your classmates do it for you?

  1. Open up Google Web Fonts in another tab.
  2. At the top of that page, click the "Word" tab.
  3. Enter the project name (below) into the "Preview Text" field.
  4. Find a font whose personality suits the project (you may want to visit that projects' page to quickly read their problem statement). Use the search and filters to find something appropriate.
  5. Finally, submit the project and font name using the form below.
  6. Refresh this page and pick fonts for at least 3 different groups.
