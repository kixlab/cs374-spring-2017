WebSIS Over the Phone: Wizard

In this activity, you will be the wizard in a formative evaluation of WebSIS Over the Phone, a touchtone phone UI for enrolling in MIT classes. As the wizard, ask the user to perform each of the three tasks below by speaking number presses to you. You should observe the user and encourage them to think aloud while they are performing the tasks.

The user is enrolled in 6.003, MAS.132, 6.813.

  1. Enroll in both 8.01 - Physics I and 3.091 - Introduction to Solid State Chemistry
  2. Change status of 6.003 - Signals and Systems to Listener
  3. Drop MAS.132 - Camera Culture

Wizard Instructions

  • Keep track of the user's enrollment at all times.
  • Directions for YOU, THE WIZARD are colored in red.
  • Directions for THE USER look like this and should be spoken by you.
  • If the user ever enters an invalid option, say Invalid option, returning to top.
  • At any time, the user can say "hang up" to restart the call.
  • Start the call using the top menu!

Top

Read the following to the user:

Welcome to WebSIS Over the Phone.

  • Press 1 to list subject enrollment
  • Press 2 to change subject status
  • Press 3 to drop a subject
  • Press 4 to add a subject

List Enrollment

Read the user's enrollment (which you should be keeping track of) then return to top.

Change Subject Status

What subject would you like to change? First, enter course number:

  • Press 1 for course 2
  • Press 2 for course 3
  • Press 3 for course 4
  • Press 4 for course 6
  • Press 5 for course 8
  • Press 6 to return to top

Note their course selection.

You have selected course course. Enter subject number.

Note their subject selection, usually a 3-digit number.

If the user is not enrolled in the subject:

You are not enrolled in course.subject. Returning to top.

Otherwise, if they are:

  • Press 1 for Listener
  • Press 2 for Credit

Note their status selection and update their enrollment.

Status of course.subject changed to status. Returning to top.

Drop a Subject

What subject would you like to drop? First, enter course number:

Wait until the user has entered all digits.
If the user enters 6-2-7, which spells out M-A-S on the keypad, the selected course is MAS.
Else if the user enters a 1 or 2 digit number, that is their selected course.

You have selected course course. Enter subject number.

Note their subject selection, usually a 3-digit number.

If the user is not enrolled in the subject:

You are not enrolled in course.subject. Returning to top.

Otherwise, if they are, drop the class in their enrollment and say:

You have dropped course.subject. Returning to top.

Add a Subject

What subject would you like to add? First, enter course number:

  • Press 1 for course 2
  • Press 2 for course 6
  • Press 3 for course 8
  • Press 4 for course 4
  • Press 5 for course 3
  • Press 6 to return to top

Note their course selection.

You have selected course course. Enter subject number.

Note their subject selection, usually a 3-digit number.

If the user is already enrolled in the subject:

You are already enrolled in course.subject. Returning to top.

Otherwise, if they aren't:

  • Press 1 for Listener
  • Press 2 for Credit

Note their status selection and update their enrollment.

You have been enrolled in course.subject, status. Returning to top.
