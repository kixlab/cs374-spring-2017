WebSIS Over the Phone: User

In this activity, you will be the user in a formative evaluation of WebSIS Over the Phone, a touchtone phone UI for enrolling in MIT classes.
As the user:

  • Perform each of the three tasks below, while thinking aloud.
  • Use the keypad as a guide, but speak your number presses to the facilitator.
  • At any time you can say 'hang up' to restart the call.

Tasks

You are enrolled in 6.003, MAS.132, 6.813.

  1. Enroll in both 8.01 - Physics I and 3.091 - Introduction to Solid State Chemistry
  2. Change status of 6.003 - Signals and Systems to Listener
  3. Drop MAS.132 - Camera Culture
